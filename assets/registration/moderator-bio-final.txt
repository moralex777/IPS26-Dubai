MODERATOR BIO - IPS26 2026
Final Version (November 10, 2025)

---

Alexander Morari, MRICS, is an experienced moderator and executive connector across real estate, institutional investment, and capital markets. His moderation expertise spans dozens of high-level conferences addressing CRE strategy, institutional capital deployment, and market infrastructure.

As CEO of PropertyTalents and Founder of RealTimes, Alexander operates at the nexus of institutional capital and real estate operators—bridging investors, asset managers, and enterprise development. He is founding an emerging VC fund, Pre-A Club, with its first fund (PAC1, $50M target) focused on the built environment. He is a member of ULI (Urban Land Institute).

Alexander advises C-suite executives on expansion strategy and business optimization, working with principals from some of Global and Europe's most significant real estate enterprises. His professional network encompasses institutional investors managing multi-billion-dollar real estate allocations and founders building transformative businesses at scale—including collaboration with executives from deca-billion enterprises.

His moderator strength: synthesizing diverse institutional perspectives—capital requirements, operational realities, market dynamics—into coherent strategic discussion that generates insight and actionable outcomes. He brings credibility across operator, investor, and advisor constituencies.

Fluent in English; MRICS qualification reflects deep engagement with real estate professional standards.
