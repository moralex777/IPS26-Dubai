## Credit Optimization Strategies (Applied)

✅ **Group Related Tasks**: Combine "speaker bio + LinkedIn + company" into single task
✅ **Clear Instructions**: Specific deliverable formats (JSON, CSV) with required fields
✅ **Avoid Re-runs**: Thorough QA review before submitting refined tasks
✅ **Use File System**: For large datasets, instruct Manus to save interim results
✅ **Monitor Progress**: Watch "Computer" interface to catch credit burn early
